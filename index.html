About Us

Welcome to Stratton Oakmont. Stratton Oakmont's journey started by delivering only horse racing books direct to people's homes with just one van around the island of Ireland. Today we are the Irish's favourite independent bookstore, providing our customers all around the world with a place to buy sport books, gift vouchers and inviting our members to all of our most recent events.

But beyond our unbelievable prices, what really sets Stratton Oakmont apart from the competition is our commitment to providing our customers with service you deserve. Whether you want to explore and shop our extensive range of discount books online, over the phone, through the post or even in the convenience of your workplace, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are on hand to offer you help and advice whenever you need it. If there is a book you are looking for but find it diffcult to find, our staff would be delighted to hear from you as we all love a challenge. You provide as much information on what you are looking for and we will do our utmost to get you that book. And that's not all... we put our money where our mouth is with our 30-day money-back guarantee - if you're not happy with your books for any reason we'll offer you a full refund, without question. We look forward to you joining us on the next chapter of our story.
